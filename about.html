About UnifiedTSN

Unified TSN is an iOS and Android networking app designed specifically for students. Our goal is to provide a problem-solving platform that helps you make friends, find tutor assistance, and even build your own business. The founder and virtually all of our team members are either students or recent graduates, so we completely understand the challenges and stresses of higher education. That's why we've developed Unified TSN - to give you the experience we wish we had while we were in school. Whether you're looking to make new connections, find support and guidance, or start your own business, Unified TSN has something to offer. Download our app today and start exploring the many benefits of student networking.

Our Goal

Our goal is to assist every student build their network of other Students, Friends, Tutors , Study buddies, Customers and even Business partners. The whole team including the founder are students and recent graduates so the problems Unified will solve are problems each and every one of us has experienced in our lives. We have used our experiences and solutions to create a platform for future students to enjoy a more interactive and student-friendly university experience. We look forward to having you join our platform!
