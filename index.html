CONNECT WITH OTHER STUDENTS, INSTANTLY

Unified lets you network with other students based on university, course or city, it's never been easier.

What is the problem?

We UK students have really seen it all haven’t we? From staring into a 400-seat lecture hall for a stranger who looks friendly enough to sit beside to internet searching ‘course tutor for hire’ because you don’t understand this particular module and it now has you questioning whether uni is really worth it.

How we can help

If I could develop a piece of technology that removes stress, I would (I’m working on it, I PROMISE!!!). Until then, here’s an app that you can register and join the community of students going through similar problems as you and are looking for a way to just enjoy this thing they call university.

What's in it for us?

we understand that university life is not always what you see in the movies and on social media, this is actually a tough sport and we want everyone to enjoy this experience and have it as comfortable as possible. My team and I believe this app is just the beginning of what Unified can provide for students and we hope you find the same value we see in it.
