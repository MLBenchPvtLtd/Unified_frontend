Key Features

Entrepreneurial shop

Or as I like to call it, Tell ‘em to bring me my money...yehhh!

Are you an entrepreneur? or just a student with a side hustle. Do you want to advertise your business on a larger scale? Open up your own business page on Unified and promote your products and services to all users on our Unified Shop. You can include your social medias, websites and other relevant URLs. Images, descriptions and prices of your products can be added to your business page to really express your entrepreneurial endeavours.

Tutoring businesses

Hey Hey! Tutors do not worry (I repeat Do Not Worry) this feature is available to all users on the app and you can also join Unified today and start gaining more student customers for your service. I needed it so I’m sure other students will too!

Filter

Again, we have come with another substitute for a stress-removing technology.

Buyers can filter businesses by city, university and type of business (beauty and healthcare, insurance, sports). These filters will help your business be seen by every customer looking regardless of whether the customer knows the name of your business or not (cuz that’s the real pain isn’t it, I need a haircut...sorry I didn’t know your business name was Amore cutz) Our filters are so precise your business will never be missing or lost in the crowd.

PSBs

Personally, I have a love/hate relationship with bios in swipe and match apps, because sometimes it does not tell the full story of why someone is on an app or the word limit restricts us from telling that story, let’s not even get into those ‘Dad- joke’ bios that aren’t even that funny. That being said, some well written bios can help find the right person to match with. Unified have added a new approach we call ‘PSBs’. PSBs are purpose-specific bios added to your profile to tell other users why you are on the app, you can have as many as you need and you can get as creative as you like with them (cool, innit?)
