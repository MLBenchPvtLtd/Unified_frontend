Privacy Policy

Welcome to Unified TSN! This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, process, and protect your personal information when you use our app and services. By accessing or using our app, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. Please take the time to read this policy carefully.

Information We Collect:

We collect certain personal information from you when you register on our app, including your full name, date of birth, email address, city, university, and course details. This information is collected through our registration form and may be updated by you through the app.

Why We Collect Your Information:

We collect this information to provide essential app functionality, such as creating user profiles and enabling user filtering. Additionally, we may use your contact information to notify you about competitions or promotions in which you may be interested.

How We Use and Process Your Data:

We may process your personal information, usage data, device information, and app activity to enhance your user experience, deliver personalized content, analyze app performance, and improve our services. Your data may also be used for marketing purposes and research to provide you with relevant content and promotions.

Sharing User Data with Third Parties:

We may share certain non-personal information, such as interests, hobbies, courses, purpose-specific bios (PSBs), shop categories, products, and services, with third parties for marketing strategies, consumer research, and targeted sales. However, rest assured that we will never share your personal information without your explicit consent.

Data Security Measures:

We prioritize the security of your data and have implemented secure servers, encryption protocols, and strict access controls managed by our administrators. We regularly update our security measures to maintain the utmost protection for your information.

Users' Rights Regarding Their Data:

As a user, you have the right to access, modify, or delete any information on your profile. If you choose to delete your account, all your data will be removed from our admin dashboard.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies:

Currently, we do not use cookies or tracking technologies. However, if we decide to implement such technologies in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Data Collection from Minors:

Unified TSN is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided personal information on our app, please contact us immediately, and we will take appropriate measures to remove such data.

International Data Transfers:

While we do not currently transfer data to third-party servers outside the user's country, we may engage in such transfers in the future. In the event of any international data transfers, we will ensure that adequate safeguards are in place to protect your data. Please note that this Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in our data practices or applicable laws. By continuing to use our app, you agree to be bound by the most recent version of this policy. If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at management@unifiedtsn.com. Thank you for choosing Unified TSN, and we look forward to providing you with a safe and enjoyable app experience.
